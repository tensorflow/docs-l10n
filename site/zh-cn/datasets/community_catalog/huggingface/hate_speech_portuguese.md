使用以下命令在 TFDS 中加载此数据集：

```python
ds = tfds.load('huggingface:hate_speech_portuguese')
```

- **说明**：

```
Portuguese dataset for hate speech detection composed of 5,668 tweets with binary annotations (i.e. 'hate' vs. 'no-hate').
```

- **版本**：1.0.0
